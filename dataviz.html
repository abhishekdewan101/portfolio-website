School Hazard Visualization Interactive map visualizing school hazards.

Project Description

The purpose of this project was to create a interactive visualization that could provide users with the relevant information about hazards that may be affecting their school districts. The users of this system were concerns parents that wanted to know the best school within a certain distance of their home for their children and politicians who were trying to close down school that were a hazard risk.

Design Process

Inital Design

As can be seen in the prototype below the visualization is quite complex and hence to plan out the different interactions that needed to be provided to the user, the team performed an inital design phase where all the various interactions were planned out before the implementation could begin.

Design Decision

The visualization allows to cater to two different kinds of users with different end goals. The user is able to rank the hazards as he/she prefers which internally affects the weightage each hazard gets in determining the best/worst school. All hazards are visually encoded to allow the user to easily percieve different hazards.
