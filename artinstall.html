Colors of Time Abstractly visualization time

Project Description

Colors of time is an art installation that can create beautiful patterns to visualize one’s weekly schedule. It consists of a frame that holds five pendulums with each pendulum representing one day of the week and an individually rotating paper base underneath the pendulums.The pendulum will then be driven to certain height according to the number of tasks user has. When releasing at the same time, the pendulums start to swing, the colorful brush-tip pens hidden in the bottom of the pendulums will hit the rotating paper base and thus creating beautiful patterns on the paper. When hitting the paper, the brush tip also gives a “tik tak” sound which is similar to the sound of a clock and it gives people a feeling of time passing.

Design Process

Brainstorming

In order to help the team decide on a theme for this project, the team went through a multiple ideation phase whereby team members were asked to come up with ideas they thought would be interesting and then debate the merits and demerits of each idea. The final idea was chosen by team consensus.

Design Mockups

Before implementing the solution, the team decided to do paper mockups of the design of the final solution. This was intended to help the team get a clear picture on the materials that were going to be used as well as refine the design of the solution before it was made permanent. The team went with a pendulum structure with a paintbrush attached to the end. The pendulum would move under the force the of gravity and using the paint brush plot lines on a revolving base.

Final Prototype

The final prototype was created using household items such as PVC pipes and plaster. The idea was to take a user's schedule and create a abstract representation of it in order to take out the inherent stress that is present within busy schedules. Moreover, the art piece could be a souvenir that the user could take with him.

Result

Each ring in the output represents a day of the week.Darker storkes represent busy days for the user while the lighter strokes represent that the user has some free time.Each day is color coded to help the user see the difference between the different days. The choice of encoding was delibrate in order to prevent interpretation of the results by any other person other than the user. As can be seen the output can be a piece of art that the user can take with him.
