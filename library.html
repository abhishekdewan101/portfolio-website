Farmington Hills Community Library Contextual Inquiry

Project Description

The Farmington hills community library requires the constat support of its volunteers to help the cost of the library to a minimum. However, during a recent staff overhaul, their volunteering hiring system became faulty which led to a shortage of volunteers. Our team was invited by the library in order to help them resolve this issue and create a better volunteering hiring workflow in order to increase the number of volunteers present at the library.

Assets

Affinity Wall

In order to consolidate the findings from the various interview, the team used a affinity wall process. The white notes represent things that were said by the users. The blue notes groups a number of white notes under a similar situation. The pink notes try to group blue notes under a single theme. The green notes consolidate the major themes across the user interviews into actionable items.

Workflow Models

During user interview it became clear that the different departments of the library weren't communicating properly with each other. Thus, the team decided to create organizational workflow models in order to understand and idenitfy the communication breakdowns within the organization. Two workflow models were considered a flow workflow model, that is able to show the flow of information within an organization and a cultural workflow model that shows the effect of heirarchy within a organization.
