Leftover's Project Meaningful interaction amongst residents of a neighbourhood

Project Description

Has it happened that you move to a new place or have lived at a place for months, but still you do not know who you neighbour is?Do you feel that you should talk to them but don’t know how? The LeftOver’s project, uses interaction design principles, to create a mobile application that allows its user to become a member of their local neighbourhood. The application is provided by the rental agencies to their patrons. This distribution methods allows to create a close knit structure when it comes to a community and also helps provide the best user experience to the target population

Design Process

Ideation

The initial ideation phase was done in order to determine the different kinds of interactions that would be made available to the user. At first I was looking at 3 different design approaches to creating the said solution. A final decision was taken to create a mobile application that would be distributed through the rental agencies. A list of features that would be made available to the user were also decided upon during this phase of the project.

Personas and Scenarios

3 personas were created keeping the target audience in mind. Each persona was created to highlight a specific portion of the final system. Moreover, the personas represented a wide variety of users ranging from cultural backgrounds to family histories. Storyboards were created for each person in order to help create a story around each persona and highlight a function within the system
