Solace Intelligently managing mental fatigue through microbreaks.

Project Description

Solace is a health informatics system that is designed to help users keep their mental fatigue in check. In high stress environments such as universities, mission critical jobs people can often fall prey to mental fatigue, sometimes without them even knowing about it, which inturn reduces their overall output in the work they are trying to do. Solace uses modular wearable devices in combination with a companion app to help the user take control of his mental fatigue in order to prevent any effects.

Design Process

Brainstorming

A large number of meeting were dedicated to brainstorming the various features that the system would track while keeping a close eye on what bodily information would help us determine the mental fatigue of a person. Moreover, design decisions about the physical form factor of the wearable were made during this phase of the project.

Inital Design Mockups

Using the insights gained during the brainstorming session, mockups of the application side of the system were created using Balsamiq. The mockups were used by the team to conduct user interviews, whereby users were shown the mockup and taken through a mock scenario and asked for feedback. The feedback would be incorporated in the final verison of the prototype.

Final Prototype

The final prototype was able to incorporate user feedback and also provide a visual overhaul to the system. Physical prototypes were also made for the wearable side of the system and all of these were demonstrated infront of a peer feedback group.
